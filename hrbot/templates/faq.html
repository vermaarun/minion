Minion - FAQs

Q - What does the bot do?

A - Minion is trained to handle day-to-day HR related tasks such as:

  • Raising a ticket.
  • Managing leaves.
  • Searching for Pramati's employee details.
Q - How can I raise a ticket?

A - You simply need to tell Minion to "Raise a ticket" and it will guide you through the process.

Q - What all leave related tasks I can do?

A - The following leave related tasks are possible through Minion

  • You can apply for a leave.
  • You can ask for the number of remaining leaves you have.
  • You can ask yet accrue leaves.
Q - How to create a leave request through minion?

A - Tell Minion to "Apply for leave" and it will ask you the relevant information and apply leave on your behalf.

Q - How can I search for an employee?

A - Ask Minion to "Search for an employee" and it will ask you the name of the employee you want to search for.

Q - What all employee details I can search?

A - You can search an employee on the basis of name. If Minion finds the employee, it will give you the details containing Name,
Employee ID, Mobile Number, Email ID and Designation.
