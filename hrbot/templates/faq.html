MINION - FAQs

What does the bot do?

Minion is trained to handle day-to-day HR related task like

  • Raising ticket
  • Managing leaves
  • Searching for Pramati employee details

How can I raise a ticket?

You simply need to tell Minion to 'Raise a ticket' and it will guide you through the process.

What all leave related tasks can I do?

The following operations are possible on Minion

  • You could ask for the number of leaves you have left
  • You could also request for a leave.

How can I create a leave request from the bot?

Tell Minion that "Apply for leave" and he would ask you for the relevant information and apply the leave on your behalf.

How can I search for an employee?

Ask Minion to "Search for an employee" and he will ask you the name of the employee you want to search for.

What details can I search for an employee?

You can search for an employee based on their name. Once the search is successful, Minion will return their Name, Employee ID, Mobile Number and Email ID, Designation.
